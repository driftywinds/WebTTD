Welcome to OpenTTD!

This is a locally built version of OpenTTD compiled to WebAssembly. You can play the full transport simulation game directly in your browser.

Game Controls:

  • Mouse: Click and drag to pan the map
  • Scroll: Zoom in/out
  • Right-click: Context menu
  • Space: Pause/unpause
  • ESC: Close dialogs

Getting Started:

  1. Click "Generate World" to create a new game
  2. Start by building roads or railways
  3. Connect cities and industries
  4. Buy vehicles to transport passengers and cargo
  5. Expand your transportation network and make profits!
